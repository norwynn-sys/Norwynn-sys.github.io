Project 2: Picture Who?

"Picture Who?" is an engaging visual puzzle game that challenges players to identify a common theme or word by analyzing a series of images. With its intuitive hint system, varying difficulty levels, and interactive feedback mechanisms, the game provides a dynamic and enjoyable experience for players of all ages. Enhanced by a captivating background music feature, Picture Who? is designed to stimulate problem-solving skills, enrich vocabulary, and offer a fun way for players to test their observational and analytical abilities.
